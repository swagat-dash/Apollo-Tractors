Seed Drill

Pneumatic Automatic Seed Drill

Apollo Seed Drill deposits seeds into the ground and covers them with soil (both ground-driven and PTO-powered models are available). Seed drills are adjustable to accommodate the required planting depth and spacing of different crops.

Features

  • Compatible with all models of Apollo tractors.
  • Break up the soil and land leveling.
  • Used in primary and secondary tillage.
  • Minimize fuel consumption and time duration.
  • Preparation of seedbed for sowing.
  • Very economical product
